Homemade Pizza

Descrption

Tasty, delicious, and all from you! This simple recipe allows you to customize your pizza however you want-- including the dough!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Bread Flour
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt & Sugar
  1. Proof the Yeast
  2. Make & Knead Pizza Dough
  3. Let Dough Rise
  4. Preheat Pizza Stone (or pan or sheet)
  5. Divide dough into two balls
  6. Prep toppings
  7. Flatten dough
  8. Brush dough with olive oil
  9. Spread with sauce and toppings
  10. Bake
  11. Voila!
