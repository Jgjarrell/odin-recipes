Baked Potato

Description

This recipe is a quick and easy way to make "baked potatoes." It is simpler and quicker than using the oven.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Desired Toppings
  1. Get similarly sized potatoes ready
  2. Put in instantpot with water
  3. Pressure cook on high for the appropriate time
  4. When done, let steam release
  5. brush with olive oil and put desired toppings on
